Built for Construction & Trades
One app that protects your margins, your crew, and your reputation on every job.
BuildLog gives PMs, foremen, and GCs a single field tool for punch lists, daily documentation, safety logging, and change order tracking — from any phone, even without signal. Everything is timestamped, assigned, and defensible.
Pain Points This Eliminates
⚖️
Change order disputes that cost you money
Without a paper trail, owners push back or claim approval never happened. The Change Orders tab timestamps every submission, captures scope, and tracks approval status — legal defensibility on every dollar.
🦺
Safety incidents with no documentation
OSHA violations and liability claims hinge on what you can prove you knew and when. The Safety Log creates a timestamped, attributed record of every hazard, near-miss, and corrective action — your first line of defense.
📋
Punch list items falling through the cracks at handoff
Paper lists get lost, photos don't attach, and the GC never has the current version. Every item here is assigned, photo-documented, and severity-flagged — so handoff is clean and warranty disputes are rare.
👷
No visibility into who's actually on site
Subcontractor no-shows cascade into schedule delays with no warning. Crew check-in status and flagged absences mean you know by 8 AM whether you have a coverage problem — before it becomes a delay claim.
📵
No internet kills documentation mid-job
Steel framing, concrete floors, and remote sites all kill cell signal. BuildLog is offline-first — all logs, photos, and punch updates capture locally and sync the moment signal returns.
🧾
Daily reports written from memory at end of week
Foremen reconstructing logs on Friday miss details that matter in disputes. The Daily Log captures crew counts, weather, decisions, and subcontractor activity in real time — searchable and exportable.
