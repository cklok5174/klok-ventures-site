Privacy Policy

How Klok Ventures collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective: June 25, 2026 Applies to: klok-ventures.com Contact: cklok5174@gmail.com
01 Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Klok Ventures ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Connor Klok, collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit klok-ventures.com (the "Site") or contact us about our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

02 Information We Collect
2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Contact us via email or a contact form (name, email address, message content)
  • Request a project quote or demo (business name, industry, project description)
  • Schedule a call through a booking tool such as Calendly (name, email, meeting preferences)
2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, basic technical information may be collected automatically by our hosting provider (Vercel), including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time of visit. We do not currently use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics tools on this Site.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect payment information directly. We do not collect sensitive personal data such as government identification numbers, health information, or financial account details through this Site.

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request
  • Send you a project quote, working demo, or proposal
  • Schedule and conduct discovery or project calls
  • Communicate updates relevant to an active project

We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent, and we do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own commercial purposes.

04 How We Store and Protect Your Information

Contact information you share with us is stored in our email system (Google Workspace) and, where applicable, in project management tools used solely for the purpose of delivering your project. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, including using encrypted email and secure cloud storage.

We retain your contact information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

05 Third-Party Services

In delivering our services, we may use the following third-party platforms, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Workspace (email and document storage) — google.com/privacy
  • Vercel (website hosting) — vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Calendly (appointment scheduling) — calendly.com/privacy
  • GitHub (code storage) — docs.github.com/privacy-policies
  • Supabase (database hosting for client projects) — supabase.com/privacy

For client projects involving data storage, we will discuss applicable third-party data processors with you prior to deployment.

06 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent to further communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at cklok5174@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

07 Children's Privacy

Our Site and services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this document. Your continued use of our Site following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
