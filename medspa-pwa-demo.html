Built for Med Spas & Wellness Studios
Turn one-time visitors into loyal clients who rebook before they leave — and refer before the month is out.
Lumière gives every client a branded portal with their upcoming visits, full treatment history, loyalty rewards, and a personalized journey — all in one app they'll actually open between appointments.
Pain Points This Eliminates
📵
Clients forget to rebook — and go somewhere else
Most spa clients intend to come back but simply forget. Without a touchpoint, they're one Instagram ad away from booking a competitor. This app stays on their phone between visits — they open it to check their appointment, browse treatments, or check their points balance. That passive presence is your most powerful retention tool.
📅
No-shows that cost $150–$400 per empty room hour
A forgotten appointment is pure lost revenue. The upcoming visit banner with live countdown and one-tap reminder keeps the appointment front of mind without staff having to call or text — and clients who set their own reminders almost never no-show.
🃏
Loyalty punch cards that end up in the trash
Paper loyalty cards get lost, forgotten, or left at home. A digital rewards program that lives on the client's phone — with a visible point balance, tier progress, and redeemable rewards — drives measurably more repeat visits. Clients who can see "860 points to Gold" come back to close the gap.
🛒
Treatment menus that live in a PDF nobody reads
A static PDF with no prices, no context, and no booking path converts nobody. Every treatment card here shows a clear description, duration, price, and a direct "Book This" button — so a client browsing at 10 PM on a Tuesday can schedule their next visit without a single phone call to your front desk.
🧠
Clients who don't know what they've had — or what to try next
When a client asks "what did I have last time?" and nobody can answer quickly, it feels disorganized. The Journey tab shows every past visit, the provider who performed it, and what's recommended next — so every appointment starts with context instead of confusion, and upsells feel like natural next steps.
📣
Referrals that happen by accident — or not at all
Word-of-mouth is your best acquisition channel, but most spas have no structured mechanism to encourage it. The loyalty program awards 500 points for every referred friend who books — creating a built-in incentive that turns happy clients into active advocates without any ad spend.
