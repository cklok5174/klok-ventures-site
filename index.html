When An App Is The Right Call

If the fix turns out to be an app, here's why I build it this way.

When a custom app genuinely is the best solution, I build what's called a Progressive Web App — it gives your customers an app-quality experience without the friction, cost, or gatekeeping of the app stores.

📵 Works Offline
Built for real-world conditions — job sites with no signal, basements, rural properties. Everything loads from cache and syncs when connectivity returns.
🔗 Installs from a Link
Send a URL by text or email. Your customer opens it in their browser and adds it to their home screen in two taps. No App Store. No Play Store. No review process.
💰 No Platform Tax
Apple and Google take 15–30% of in-app purchases and charge $99/year just to exist. A PWA cuts them out entirely — you keep everything your customers pay.
⚡ Fast to Build & Change
Native apps take months and cost tens of thousands. A PWA goes from brief to working product remarkably fast — and updates deploy instantly, no app store review required.
📱 Any Device, Any Screen
One build works on iPhone, Android, tablet, and desktop. Your field crew uses it on Android. Your clients use it on iPhone. Same app, same experience.
🎨 Fully Branded
Every pixel is yours. Your name, your colors, your voice. Not a white-labeled SaaS tool with someone else's logo in the corner — a product that looks like you built it.
